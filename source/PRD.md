# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## NAWAB GOAT FARM – Website

---

## 1. Overview

### Product Name
**NAWAB GOAT FARM – Official Website**

### Tagline
**Where goats are treated with pleasure**

### Purpose
The website will act as an **informational and contact platform** for NAWAB GOAT FARM.  
Its primary goal is to showcase goat breeds, farm values, and services (including goat hiring for Palai), while making it easy for customers to contact the farm via WhatsApp, email, and social media.

---

## 2. Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goals
- Establish a professional online presence for NAWAB GOAT FARM
- Showcase available goat breeds and farm practices
- Provide clear contact channels for inquiries
- Build trust using visuals (gallery + video)

### Secondary Goals
- Increase reach via social media (YouTube + Facebook)
- Ensure accessibility on both mobile and desktop devices

---

## 3. Non-Goals (Out of Scope)

The website **must NOT include**:
- Online payments
- E-commerce functionality
- User login or admin panel
- Goat pricing or booking system
- CMS or backend dashboard

This is a **static / content-driven website** with basic interactivity only.

---

## 4. Target Audience

- Goat buyers
- Farmers
- People looking to hire goats for **Palai**
- Local and regional customers
- Mobile-first users (rural and semi-urban areas)

---

## 5. Pages & Functional Requirements

### 5.1 Home Page

**Purpose:**  
First impression and brand introduction.

**Content Requirements:**
- Brand name: **NAWAB GOAT FARM**
- Tagline: *Where goats are treated with pleasure*
- Short description:  
  > We deal in all types of breed of goats. Here goats are hired for Palai.
- Call-to-action buttons:
  - Contact on WhatsApp
  - View Goat Breeds
- Highlight sections:
  - Goat Breeds (preview)
  - Gallery preview
  - Embedded YouTube video
- Social links:
  - Facebook page link

---

### 5.2 About Page

**Purpose:**  
Explain the farm’s values and credibility.

**Content Requirements:**
- Introduction to NAWAB GOAT FARM
- Farming philosophy and care standards
- Emphasis on ethical treatment of goats
- Experience and reliability
- Palai hiring explanation (what it is and how it works)

---

### 5.3 Goat Breeds Page (or Sections)

**Purpose:**  
Showcase different goat breeds available.

**Content Requirements:**
- List or sections for goat breeds
- Each breed section should include:
  - Breed name
  - Short description
  - One or more images
- No pricing
- No availability status
- Informational content only

---

### 5.4 Gallery Page

**Purpose:**  
Build trust and authenticity through visuals.

**Content Requirements:**
- Images of:
  - Goats
  - Farm environment
  - Palai-related visuals (if available)
- Responsive image grid
- Optimized loading for mobile users

---

### 5.5 Contact Page

**Purpose:**  
Enable easy communication.

**Contact Methods Required:**
- **WhatsApp Contact**
  - Click-to-chat button
- **Email Contact Form**
  - Fields:
    - Name
    - Phone (optional)
    - Email
    - Message
- **Social Links**
  - Facebook page
- Optional:
  - Location text (no map required)

---

## 6. Features & Functional Requirements

### 6.1 Responsive UI
- Fully responsive design
- Optimized for:
  - Mobile phones
  - Tablets
  - Desktop screens

---

### 6.2 WhatsApp Integration
- Floating or fixed WhatsApp button
- Opens chat with predefined message
- Mobile and desktop compatible

---

### 6.3 Email Contact Form
- Simple form submission
- Sends message to farm’s email
- Basic validation (required fields)

---

### 6.4 YouTube Video Embed
- Embedded YouTube video on Home or Gallery page
- Used for:
  - Farm tour
  - Goat showcase
- Responsive embed

---

### 6.5 Facebook Page Link
- Clickable Facebook icon or link
- Opens official Facebook page in a new tab

---

## 7. Content Requirements

### Fixed Text Content
- Brand Name: **NAWAB GOAT FARM**
- Tagline: *Where goats are treated with pleasure*
- Description:  
  > We deal in all types of breed of goats. Here goats are hired for Palai.

### Language
- Simple, clear English
- Easy to understand for a broad audience

---

## 8. UX & Design Guidelines

- Clean and rural-friendly design
- Natural colors (green, brown, white)
- Large readable fonts
- Minimal animations
- Clear navigation menu:
  - Home
  - About
  - Goat Breeds
  - Gallery
  - Contact

---

## 9. Technical Requirements

- Static website (HTML/CSS/JS or framework-based)
- Fast loading on low-bandwidth networks
- SEO-friendly structure
- Image optimization
- No backend database required

---

## 10. Success Metrics

- Users can:
  - View goat breeds easily
  - Contact via WhatsApp within one click
  - Submit contact form successfully
- Website works smoothly on mobile devices
- Social media links function correctly
