What is Palai?

Palai is a traditional goat boarding/hiring service where healthy goats are given to farmers for breeding and care. The goats are fed, maintained, and bred at the farmer's location, and the benefits are shared between the farm and the caretaker.

At NAWAB GOAT FARM, we provide premium goats for Palai with full veterinary support, ensuring healthy breeding and profitable returns for both parties.
